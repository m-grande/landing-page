About Odin

God of War and Death

Half of the warriors who die in battle are taken to his majestic hall, Valhalla, by the Valkyries.

God of Wisdom and Knowledge

This is a defining trait. He famously sacrificed one of his eyes to drink from Mimir's Well, gaining immense knowledge of the cosmos.

God of Poetry and Magic

He is associated with runic magic, prophecy (seidr), and poetic inspiration.
